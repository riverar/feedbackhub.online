Skype Preview App Update: version 11.10 for PC and Mobile

Applicable Rings: Fast Store update

We’ve made a number of improvements to the Skype Preview app with today’s flight to Windows Insiders – including:

  • Voice translation on calls to mobile and landlines with Skype Translator
  • Introduce friends and family on Skype by sharing their Skype contact details
  • Capture and share video messages with friends and family on Skype – even if they’re not online
  • Manage your conversations and save time by marking conversations as read or unread
  • Never miss another call by forwarding them to any mobile or landline

You can learn more here on the Skype Blog – looking forward to your feedback!
