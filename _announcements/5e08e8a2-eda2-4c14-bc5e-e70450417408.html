Groove Music app: version 10.16102.1021

Applicable Ring: Fast Ring Store update

  • Re-order songs in playlists by using the keyboard, not just drag-and-drop. When you put focus on a single track, you’ll notice new Move Up and Move Down options in the context menu and in the app bar.
  • We got feedback that users couldn’t FF/RW/Seek in songs when the window was small. We updated this so you can seek in more window sizes.
  • Deleting items from my collection won't remove them from Recent Plays.
  • Improved reliability of downloading, deleting, syncing, and fetching album metadata.
  • Improved context provided when navigating the app with the screen reader turned on.
