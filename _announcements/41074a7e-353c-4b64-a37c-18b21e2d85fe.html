Applicable Rings: Fast, Slow, and Release Preview

Use Ringtone Maker to create ringtones from your favorite sounds in only three steps:

  1. Open the app
  2. Select a sound file
  3. Save it as a ringtone.

You can also edit your ringtone to choose the start, end and length of your ringtone.

Ringtone Maker supports wma, mp3 and m4a file formats.
