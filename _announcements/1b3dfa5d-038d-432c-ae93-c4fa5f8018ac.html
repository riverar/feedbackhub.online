Feedback Hub App: 1.1609.2801.0 for PC and Mobile

Applicable Ring: Fast Ring Store update

You can now read and comment on both mobile and PC feedback, whether you're using your PC or mobile device

We are happy to announce that now you can reach and comment on both mobile and PC feedback, whether you're using a PC or mobile device.

We introduced the new feature to let users:

  • Search and browse feedback from all form factors regardless of your current device form factor. For example, you can now see Mobile from Desktop
  • Open feedback from any form factor via URI or links
  • Comment on any feedback item regardless of form factor

 

You can now easily switch between your Microsoft accounts while in Feedback Hub

You can now easily switch accounts in Feedback Hub and sign in with different MSAs. This helps with:

  • Being able to easily see Insider or Retail views
  • Use a registered MSA to sign in if it is different than the MSA used to sign in to OS
