3D Builder App: Version 11.1.7.0

  • New Office-style interface
    • We moved our tools to a Ribbon, to make them simpler to understand.
  • Build new 3D objects from scratch
    • You can now start with an empty stage and drag-and-drop shapes from the Insert tab to build.
  • Multiple windows
    • Open as many 3D Builder windows as you want! Do side-by-side editing or viewing.
  • Print on paper
    • Print a picture of your creation to paper or to PDF.
  • New step-by-step print dialog experience
    • The print dialog guides you in making the perfect 3D Print. You can either use your personal 3D printer or have a professional print mailed to you.
