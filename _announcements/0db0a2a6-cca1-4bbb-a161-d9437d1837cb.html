Skype App version 11.11.103.0

Applicable Ring: Windows Insider Slow

  • You can now use the unread counter on the Skype Preview tile to easily see how many unseen conversations on that machine you have.
  • If you have enabled the “Use my address book” setting on your local device, you can send a contact request via email to contacts on your device.
