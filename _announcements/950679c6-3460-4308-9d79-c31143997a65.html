Come join members of Windows and Devices Group Engineering team in select Microsoft Stores from June 27-July 2nd to learn more about the Windows 10 Anniversary Update and the Windows Insider Program. At each Microsoft Store on the tour we will be hosting a series of events that we welcome all Windows Insiders, friends, family, or anyone that just wants to learn more about the Windows 10 Anniversary Update or the Windows Insider Program.

Windows Insider – Windows 10 Anniversary Update Demo

Stop by if you are interested in getting an early look at the Windows 10 Anniversary Update, learn about becoming a Windows Insider, and potentially trying out the Windows 10 Anniversary Update preview builds on your own Windows 7/Windows 8.x/Windows 10 PC or supported Windows 10 Mobile device. You will have the opportunity to:

  • See demos of what is new in Windows 10 Anniversary Update
  • Learn more about the Windows Insider Program
  • Ways to provide feedback on how we can make better products for you
  • Work hands on with engineers to get onsite support on your own device
  • Install the latest Insider Preview builds on your own PC, laptop, or supported phone.
    • Be sure to bring in your PC, laptop, or phone to the Microsoft Store and we will have people onsite to help!

Happy Hour with the Windows and Devices Engineering Team*

Join us for an informal meet-up with the Windows Engineering Team. We will have Windows Engineers onsite to meet with you, answer your questions, and share each of our own favorite pieces of the Windows 10 Anniversary Update. Snacks will be provided and of course bring your device along if you want any help getting started with the Windows Insider Program or any other help.

*This is an all ages event and alcohol will not be served, sorry.
