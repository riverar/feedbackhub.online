Today, Microsoft is launching Office Insider, an ongoing preview program for consumer Office subscribers (Office 365 Home, Office 365 Personal and Office 365 University) across platforms and devices.  As an Office Insider, you will get first access to the latest innovations – putting you on the inside track for all things Office.

As Windows Insiders, you already have access to the Insider versions of Office Mobile apps for Windows 10.  You are all set!

If you are an Office 365 subscriber, you should join now!

Interested in learning more about what’s new for Office 365 subscribers? Check out the Office Blog.
